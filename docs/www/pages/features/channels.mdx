The Channels feature creates topic-based discussion spaces for focused community interaction.

## Overview

Channels organize conversations by:
- Subject matter or topics
- Course-specific discussions
- Interest-based communities
- Project collaboration spaces

## Usage

Channels provide structured spaces for discussions, allowing users to join conversations relevant to their interests and courses.
